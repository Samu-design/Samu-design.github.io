Cinnamon Rolls

Cinnamon rolls are sweet, spiraled pastries filled with cinnamon-sugar and topped with frosting or glaze. Popular for their comforting flavor and versatility, they come in classic, sticky, or creative variations, making them a timeless treat for any occasion.

Éclairs & Profiteroles

Éclairs are long pastries filled with cream or custard and topped with glaze, while profiteroles are round, bite-sized pastries filled with cream or ice cream and often drizzled with chocolate or caramel. Both are light, versatile French treats perfect for special occasions.

Cheesecakes

Cheesecake is a rich dessert made from cream cheese, a crumbly crust, and various toppings. Available in baked or no-bake forms, its variations range from dense New York-style to light Japanese-style. Cheesecakes are versatile, customizable, and perfect for any occasion.
